Inspired by the popular game played on the Football Cliches podcast.
The Rules
  • The aim of the game is to guess goalscorers for teams at away stadiums in Premier League history.
  • There are three rounds, where you will be given a Premier League team and a ground combination to guess.
  • You must choose a difficulty level between 1-10. 1 being the easiest, 10 being the hardest.
  • You must answer with a player that you think they scored at that ground for that team, as well as a season you think they scored in.
  • If you get the player and season correct, you score points equal to the level you chose.
  • If you choose a player that has scored at that stadium for that team, but not in the season you chose, you score half the points of the level you chose.
Notes
  • This is not affiliated with the Football Cliches podcast. This has been created by a fan of the pod.
  • The difficulty level is based on the number of possible unique answer combinations for that team and stadium, and how recent the most recent answer is.
  • A Level 1 example might be Manchester United at Goodison Park, the combination with the most unqiue goalscorers.
  • A Level 10 example might be Manchester City at Boundary Park, where there is only one correct goalscorer.
  • Data is accurate as of the end of the 2023/34 season. The 2024/25 season will be added some time after the conclusion of the season.
  • Disclaimer: I do not own the rights to the Premier League player headshots, these are hosted on the Premier League's website.
